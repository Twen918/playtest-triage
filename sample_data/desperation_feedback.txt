# Desperation playtest feedback - Fall 2025 sessions (one note per line)
The monster pulled me out of the locker right after I hid - I didn't even get a chance. Feels unfair.
Game is way too dark, I couldn't find the fuse room even with the flashlight.
Sliding to escape the monster feels amazing.
I had no idea how long my speed boost would last, there's no timer on screen.
The footstep sound effect kept looping after I stopped moving in the packaged build.
Frame rate tanks in the freezer room when the fog kicks in.
The keypad puzzle was great once I realized the code was hidden in the notes.
I couldn't tell which key opened which door, took me forever.
Genuinely the scariest student game I've played, the atmosphere is incredible.
Monster speed feels too fast in the early game before you get any power-ups.
My objective marker disappeared after I died and reloaded.
The wire puzzle in generator room 2 didn't register my last connection once.
Inspection system is cool but I wish I could rotate the notes in 3D.
Crouching noise level isn't obvious - am I quiet right now or not?
I got stuck on a box in the storage room and had to restart.
Love the bloodscreen effect when you take damage.
The final chase was intense but one wrong turn killing you instantly felt cheap.
Vision power-up slows you down too much to be useful.
